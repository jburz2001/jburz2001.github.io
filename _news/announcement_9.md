I was accepted to attend the [Joint IAEA/ENEA/EMFCSC Fusion School](https://conferences.iaea.org/event/469/) in Erice, Italy from 06/07/2026--06/12/2026. The school will be hosted at the historic [Ettore Majorana Foundation and Centre for Scientific Culture
(EMFCSC)](https://ettoremajoranafoundation.com/) and its [curriculum](https://conferences.iaea.org/event/469/attachments/22023/37867/Information%20for%20Participants%20Erice-Fusion%202026.pdf) will cover plasma physics, tokamak engineering, environmental considerations, and more!

I give my thanks to those at the [U.S. Burning Plasma Organization](https://burningplasma.org/) for supporting and sponsoring this oppurtunity.
