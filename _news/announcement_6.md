I was invited to present at a <a href="https://ucleads.ucop.edu/">UC LEADS</a> workshop on 08/05/2025 on how to interweave stories of adverse life experiences into graduate school applications.
\\
As a former foster youth, I find it important to effectively convey triumph over adversity, as there is usually a negative perception of this demographic. Unfortunately, <a href="https://publichealth.berkeley.edu/articles/spotlight/students/from-foster-care-to-public-health-changemakers"><4% of foster youth obtain a B.S. degree and <1% obtain an M.S.</a> compared to <a href="https://www.census.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2022/educational-attainment.html">>37% and >14%</a> of the U.S. population over 25 in 2022. So few foster youth obtain a Ph.D. that I could not find a statistical quantification---I assume it’s <0.05%.
